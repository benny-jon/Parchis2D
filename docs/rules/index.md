# Parchis 2D — Official Rules

**Parchis 2D** is a classic race board game for 2–4 players, designed for local play with friends and family.  

These rules are based on traditional **Spanish Parchis / Parcheesi** gameplay.

---

## 1. Objective

Be the **first player** to move all **four pieces** from your **Start area**, around the board, and into your **Home**.

---

## 2. Number of Players

- 2, 3, or 4 players
- Each player controls one color

---

## 5. Dice and Turns

On your turn:
1. Roll both dice.
2. You may:
   - Move **one piece** using the total of both dice, **or**
   - Move **two different pieces**, one per die (if legal).
3. If at least one legal move exists, you must make a move.

---

## 6. Leaving Start

- A piece must leave **Start** only if you roll a **5** or both dice combined add up to **5**. Ex: 2 + 3, 4 + 1, etc.
- If you roll a 5 there is an enemy blocade in your Start, you cannot Start.

---

## 7. Rolling Doubles

- Rolling **doubles** grants an **extra turn**
- If doubles are rolled **three times in a row**:
  - The most advanced piece is sent back to **Start**
  - The turn ends immediately

---

## 8. Movement Rules

- Pieces move **counter-clockwise**
- Pieces must land **exactly** on Home (no overshooting)
- Pieces may not pass through:
  - Any **blockade**
- If no legal move exists, the turn is skipped

---

## 9. Safe Tiles

Safe tiles are special squares where pieces **cannot be captured**.

### Safe Tile Rules:
- Pieces on safe tiles **cannot be captured**
- **Two pieces of different colors may share the same safe tile**
- Sharing a safe tile with different colors:
  - ✅ Is allowed
  - ❌ Does **not** form a blockade
- If you have a piece on an enemy Start tile, your piece can be **captured** if the enemy starts 2 pieces.

---

## 10. Blockades

A **blockade** is formed when:
- Two pieces of the **same color** occupy the same tile

### Blockade Rules:
- No piece may pass through a blockade (including the owner’s)
- Blockades may exist on:
  - Regular tiles
  - Safe tiles (same color only)
- A blockade must be broken when rolling a double.

❗ Two pieces of **different colors never form a blockade**, even on safe tiles.

---

## 11. Capturing Pieces

A capture occurs when:
- A piece lands on a tile occupied by **one opponent piece**
- The tile is **not** a safe tile

### Capture Effects:
- The captured piece is returned to its **Start**
- The capturing player earns a **bonus move of 20 spaces**

---

## 12. Bonus Moves

Bonus movement is awarded for:
- **Capturing a piece** → Move 20 spaces
- **Moving a piece into Home** → Move 10 spaces

Bonus rules:
- Bonus moves are forfeited if no legal move exists

---

## 13. Home Path and Home

- Each player has a color-specific Home path
- Only that player’s pieces may enter it
- Pieces must land **exactly** on Home

Once a piece reaches Home:
- It is removed from play
- It cannot be captured or moved again

---

## 14. No Legal Moves

If:
- All moves are blocked by blockades
- Moves would overshoot Home
- No pieces can leave Start

Then:
- The turn is skipped

---

## 15. Winning the Game

- The first player to move **all four pieces into Home** wins.
- Optionally, play may continue to determine remaining placements.

---

## 17. Rule Clarifications Summary

- ✅ Two different colors may share a safe tile
- ❌ Different colors do NOT form blockades
- ❌ Captures cannot occur on safe tiles
- ❌ Blockades cannot be passed

---

Enjoy playing **Parchis 2D**!
