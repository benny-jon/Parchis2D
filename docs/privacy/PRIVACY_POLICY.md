# Privacy Policy — Parchis 2D (Unity Game)

**Last updated:** 2026-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how **Parchis 2D** (the “App”) handles information when you use it.

---

## Summary (Plain English)

- **We do not collect or sell personal information.**
- The App is designed to work **offline** for local play with friends/family.
- Any game progress/settings are stored **on your device** (if you choose to save them).
- If you contact us, we only use what you send to respond.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1) Information You Provide
The App does **not** require you to create an account.

If you email us for support, we may receive:
- Your email address
- The contents of your message
- Any screenshots/logs you choose to share

We use this only to provide support.

### 2) Information Collected Automatically
**None by default.**  
The App does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

> If we add analytics/crash reporting in the future, we will update this policy and disclose the provider(s) and what data they collect.

### 3) On-Device Data (Local Storage)
The App may store the following **locally on your device**:
- Game settings (e.g., sound, language)
- Local game state or preferences (if implemented)

This data is not transmitted to us.

---

## How We Use Information
- To operate the App features on your device
- To respond to support requests (only if you contact us)

---

## Sharing of Information
We do not share personal information with third parties because we do not collect it.

If you contact support, we may share your message internally only as needed to respond.

---

## Children’s Privacy
The App is a family-friendly board game experience.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.  
If you believe a child has provided personal information to us by email, contact us and we will delete it.

---

## Data Retention
- **On-device game data** remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.
- **Support emails** are kept only as long as needed to respond and maintain basic records.

---

## Your Choices
- You can delete on-device data by uninstalling the App (or clearing app storage).
- You can request deletion of support communications by contacting us.

---

## Security
We use reasonable measures to protect any communications you send us (e.g., support emails).  
However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

---

## International Users
If you contact us from outside the United States, your message may be processed where we are located.

---

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
We will post the updated version in this repository and update the **“Last updated”** date above.
